The Weed Drag

Clearer Mind, Better Life

We'll be honest. Quitting marijuana is tough; especially the first couple of weeks. Thankfully, the benefits start piling up quickly. And this can give you the motivation you need to keep going.

Here's what happens from the moment you stop using marijuana.

24 Hours

Appetite decreases and mood swings may occur as your brain restores natural dopamine production.
